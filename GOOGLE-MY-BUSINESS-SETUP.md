## Important Tips

1. **Complete your profile 100%** - Fully completed profiles rank higher
2. **Get reviews consistently** - Aim for 3-5 new reviews per month
3. **Respond quickly** - Fast response time improves ranking
4. **Post regularly** - At least 2-3 posts per week
5. **Use keywords** - Include "Ranchi cab service", "taxi in Ranchi" in description
6. **Add photos regularly** - Add new photos every week
7. **Use Google Posts** - Posts appear in search results
8. **Verify location** - Even for service area businesses
9. **Monitor Q&A** - Answer questions promptly
10. **Track insights** - See what's working
